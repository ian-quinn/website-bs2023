BS2023 Shanghai, China

Happy New Year!

Dear colleagues,

The organizers of the Building Simulation Conference 2023 wish you a happy new year!

China will lift quarantine for inbound travelers from January 8, marking the last major shift from the zero-COVID policy. We have high hopes for a physical BS2023, and look forward to meet all our colleagues in Shanghai.

People arriving in China from abroad for work, business, study and family reunions, will no longer be subjected to any quarantine or other restrictions. A cap on the daily number of flights allowed into China would also be scraped. The tourism visa policies would be adjusted very soon. We will keep you updated on the policy page.

BS2023 remains to be a HYBRID event. However, we strongly recommend that you join us in person for an unforgettable reunion. With many exciting things in store, Shanghai awaits your discovery. See you soon!
